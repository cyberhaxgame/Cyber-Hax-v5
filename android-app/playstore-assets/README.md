## Play Store Asset Templates

These SVGs are editable source assets for your Play listing.

- `cyber-hax-store-icon.svg`
  Export this to a `512x512` PNG for the Play Store app icon.
- `cyber-hax-feature-graphic.svg`
  Export this to a `1024x500` PNG for the feature graphic.

You can open these in Figma, Illustrator, Inkscape, or most browser-based SVG editors.
